ABOUT THE COMPANY

«PINOFLOT» has been operating since 1988, specializing in the production of polyurethane foam products. A wide range of products and a wide range of uses of polyurethane foam will give you the opportunity to choose what you need.

Our company produces:

  • Pipes pre-insulated with polyurethane foam in a polyethylene or galvanized shell for laying heating mains;
  • Polyurethane foam segments for thermal insulation of pipelines;
  • Plates polyurethane foam heat-insulating of any thickness and density;
  • Products from elastic polyurethane foam of cold forming (seats and backs for the furniture industry, automobile, railway, aviation, water transport);
  • We perform work on thermal insulation by spraying and pouring of polyurethane foam (industrial and technological equipment, vegetable storages, fruit storages, buildings, constructions from any building materials);
